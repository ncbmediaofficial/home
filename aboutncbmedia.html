About NCB Media

Where our team meets to create transformative background for every artiest in the globe

Our Story

NCB Media is a discovery and promotion platform for musicians — a place where fans, industry professionals, and curious listeners can search for an artist, read their story, watch videos, follow their social channels, see upcoming shows, and jump straight to their music on every major streaming platform.

We launched about three years ago with a clear mission: to make artist stories easy to find, richly documented, and shareable. To kick things off, we chose Shatta Wale as our first artist and Governor — a role he embodies as a major influence and role model for our team. But NCB Media is for every musician: male and female, established and emerging, across all genres. We publish regular updates, features, and promotional stories to help artists get discovered and to help fans follow the creators they love.

    What we offer

  • Searchable, authoritative artist profiles and bios
  • Embedded videos and curated video playlists
  • Direct links to an artist’s social media and streaming platforms
  • Up-to-date calendars of live shows and events
  • Featured stories, interviews, and promotional support for musicians

Our team

NCB Media was founded and is led by Adam Rafiu Timtooni, together with Adam Amin, Adam Mashud, and Adam Abdul Manan. We’re music lovers and storytellers — and we built this platform to put artists and their work front and center.

Our Core Values

💡

Mission

NCB Media’s mission is to make music discovery effortless and meaningful. We document and promote artists’ careers—across genres and stages—by providing authoritative profiles, curated videos, direct streaming links, and up-to-date event information. Through honest storytelling, wide accessibility, and community-driven promotion, we help musicians reach new listeners and turn fans into supporters.

🚀

Vision

Our vision is to transform how the world finds and celebrates music by making artist stories, media, and events effortlessly discoverable in one authoritative place. We aim to bridge gaps between creators and audiences, uplift emerging talent alongside established names, and foster a vibrant, inclusive community where music careers can thrive. Through thoughtful storytelling, smart tools, and partnerships across the industry, NCB Media will help shape the future of music discovery and fan engagement.

❤️

Our Values

At NCB Media we believe in an artist-first, inclusive approach to music discovery. We combine accurate storytelling and community collaboration with accessible, technology-driven tools to make every artist’s profile, media, and events easy to find. Guided by integrity and a deep passion for music, we promote talent across genres and career stages—helping artists connect with fans and grow their careers.
